Time Spent Startup

2020 In Progress

Python

Time Spent is a startup building a platform that helps connect people and learn specific skills (e.g., languages).

Specifically for immigrants and refugees with a low-English proficiency level, we want to provide an online space in which, low-English proficiency immigrants and refugees can meet volunteers to practice English conversation, receive help with understanding paper forms, and ask questions they have about American culture and customs.
